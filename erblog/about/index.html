About Masoud Kalali

2016-02-01

I have been working on variety of software development and sometimes software-development social projects and engagements for a long while now.

Masoud has been working on software development projects since 1998, which gives him a broad perspective on software development in general and changes in the software development landscape in the past 1/5 decades. Masoud has experience with variety of technologies (.NET, J2EE, CORBA, and COM+) on diverse platforms (Solaris, Linux, and Windows). He has a master degree in information systems with a bachelor degree in software engineering.

Masoud has authored fair number of articles and other types of publications. He is author of multiple refcards, published by Dzone, including but not limited to:

Masoud’s main area of research and interest includes service-oriented architecture and large-scale systems’ development and deployment. In his spare time he enjoys photography, mountaineering and climbing. P.S. Masoud is a Consulting Member of Technical Staff at ORACLE.
